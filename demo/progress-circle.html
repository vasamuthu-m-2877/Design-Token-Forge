Accessibility

A11y

role="progressbar" with ARIA value attributes. Forced-colors and reduced-motion supported.

ARIA Attributes

Determinate: aria-valuenow + aria-valuemin + aria-valuemax. Indeterminate: omit aria-valuenow, add aria-label.

Disabled

data-disabled reduces opacity to 50% and removes pointer events.

Reduced Motion

Indeterminate spin and fill transitions stop. Static ring only. prefers-reduced-motion: reduce.

Forced Colors

Track stroke becomes CanvasText. Fill stroke becomes Highlight. SVG structure preserved.
