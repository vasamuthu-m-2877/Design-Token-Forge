Why this is the Foundation-tier proof slice

First L2 in @dtf/foundation-mobile · validates the tier per v0.2 architecture.

  • Compositional: built from existing L1 icon-button + text — zero new L1 atoms needed for this slice.
  • Role inheritance flows through: the L1s inside re-route their --brand-* tokens automatically; no token re-wiring at the L2 level.
  • 50 tokens vs L1 button's 246: Foundation L2s are intentionally lighter — they inherit heavy lifting from L1s.
  • 3 density modes vs L1's 10: opinionated. Foundations narrow the surface; shape-kits never need more.
  • Used in 4 kits (Writing, Commerce, Reading, Chat) — passes the Foundation qualifier #1 (≥2 shape-kits).
  • Intent stable across all 4 — passes qualifier #2.
  • RTL ready via logical properties + dir=rtl selectors.
  • Safe-area aware via env(safe-area-inset-top).
