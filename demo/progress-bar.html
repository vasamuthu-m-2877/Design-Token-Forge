Accessibility

A11y

role="progressbar" with aria-valuenow, aria-valuemin, aria-valuemax. Forced-colors and reduced-motion are supported.

ARIA Attributes

Determinate bars: aria-valuenow + aria-valuemin + aria-valuemax. Indeterminate: omit aria-valuenow.

Disabled

data-disabled reduces opacity to 50% and removes pointer events.

Reduced Motion

Stripe and indeterminate animations stop. Static fill only. prefers-reduced-motion: reduce.

Forced Colors

Track gets CanvasText border. Fill uses Highlight. Stripe pattern uses system colors.
