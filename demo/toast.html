Accessibility

A11y

ARIA roles, keyboard navigation, forced colors, and reduced motion behavior.

ARIA Live Regions

Info/success/warning use role="status" (polite). Danger uses role="alert" (assertive). Screen readers announce toast content automatically.

Keyboard

Close button and action button are focusable and tabbable. Escape can be wired to dismiss all. Focus ring via :focus-visible.

Forced Colors

High Contrast mode: border falls back to CanvasText. Accent bar and progress bar use Highlight for visibility.

Reduced Motion

When prefers-reduced-motion: reduce is active, all transitions and animations resolve to near-instant (0.01ms).
