Status Badges

4 Statuses

Online, offline, busy, and away indicators. Badge scales proportionally with avatar size (25%).

Avatar Group

Composition

Overlapping avatars with ring border. Offset = -25% of avatar size. Includes count overflow.

Slots & Anatomy

4 Layers

Image, initials, icon fallback, and status badge. Each layer fills the container. Badge positions bottom-right.

Motion

2 Animations

Skeleton loading shimmer and online badge pulse. Reduced motion disables all animations.

Accessibility

A11y

Image alt, ARIA labeling, focus ring, and high contrast support.

Image Alt

Image avatars require alt with user's name. Screen readers announce the image content.

Initials ARIA

Use role="img" and aria-label="Full Name" on the avatar container. Initials alone aren't meaningful to assistive tech.

Focus Ring

Interactive avatars (links, buttons) show focus-visible outline. Width and color are tokenised.

Forced Colors

High Contrast shows ButtonText border on all avatars. Badge border uses Canvas for cutout effect.
