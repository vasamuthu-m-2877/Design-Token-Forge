Slots & Anatomy

Slots

6 slot configurations. Accent bar, icon, title, body (with links), close button — all optional. Icon and close sizes scale with the density token.

Accent Bar

Shape

Left-border accent bar. Width: --alert-accent-width (4px). Radius: --alert-accent-radius (radius-full). Color: --_al-accent → --{role}-component-bg-default. Hidden on filled and soft variants.

Dismiss

Motion

Entry via @keyframes alert-enter (slide-down + fade), dismiss via @keyframes alert-dismiss (slide-up + fade-out). Tokens: --alert-animation-enter, --alert-animation-dismiss. Click × to dismiss, then reset.

Accessibility

A11y

ARIA roles (role="alert" for danger/warning, role="status" for info), keyboard navigation, forced colors, and reduced motion behavior.

Focus Ring

Close button shows :focus-visible ring via --alert-focus-outline-width (2px) + --alert-focus-outline-color → --brand-component-outline-default.

Disabled

data-disabled applies --alert-disabled-opacity (0.5) and pointer-events:none. Close button becomes inert.

Reduced Motion

When prefers-reduced-motion: reduce is active, dismiss transition resolves to near-instant (0.01ms). Shimmer animations removed.

Forced Colors

High Contrast mode: border falls back to CanvasText. Accent bar uses Highlight. All role colors collapse to system palette.
