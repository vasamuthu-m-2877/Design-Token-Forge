Accessibility

A11y

ARIA pairing, keyboard, forced colors, and reduced motion.

ARIA Pairing

Trigger uses aria-describedby → tooltip id. Tooltip has role="tooltip". Screen readers announce tooltip content when trigger is focused.

Keyboard

Show on focus, hide on Escape. Tooltip has pointer-events: none so it never traps focus or steals clicks.

Forced Colors

Border falls back to CanvasText. Arrow background uses CanvasText for visibility in High Contrast mode.

Reduced Motion

Scale/opacity transition resolves to near-instant (0.01ms) when prefers-reduced-motion: reduce is active.
