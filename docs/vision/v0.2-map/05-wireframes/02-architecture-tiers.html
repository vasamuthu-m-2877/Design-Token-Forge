The new Foundation tier captures the ~77% of L2 patterns that are platform-shape concerns (being a mobile app) rather than product-shape concerns (being a writing app). It sits between L1 and shape-kits.

Build math — why the Foundation tier is mandatory

Probe 2 measured 77% L2 overlap between Mobile Writing and Mobile Commerce. Without a Foundation tier, every shape-kit re-ships the same 10 patterns — paying 4× the cost across 4 kits. The Foundation tier captures them once.

Without Foundation tier · v0.1

4 mobile kits × ~14 L2s × ~4 states each = 216 component-states
Writing · 54
Commerce · 54
Reading · 54
Chat · 54
216 states. Every kit re-ships AppShell, TopBar, BottomTabBar… every time. ~40 of those states are duplicates.

With Foundation tier · v0.2

Foundation 40 + Writing 16 + Commerce 24 + Reading 12 + Chat 16 = 108 states
Foundation · 40 (shared)
Writing +16
Commerce +24
Reading +12
Chat +16
108 states. 50% reduction. Foundation pays back after the 2nd shape-kit.

Governance — who owns what

Tier Owner Versioning Contribution
L0 + L1 DTF core independent semver RFC + tests
Foundation new DTF core independent semver · shape-kits pin major RFC · must be used in ≥2 shape-kits
Shape Kit DTF core → shape teams pinned to Foundation major open · shape teams own
L2 / L3 product designer per-product recipe library
Promotion path: shape-specific L2 → second use in different shape → RFC → Foundation candidate → reviewed → promoted on next minor. Mirrors the recipes→L2 rule one tier up.

Open questions surfaced by this tier

  • Are Desktop and Marketing Foundations real, or is Mobile a special case? Tested only on mobile so far.
  • Foundation LTS — how long do shape-kits get to migrate after a major Foundation bump? Suggested: 6 months.
  • Cross-platform Foundations — do some primitives (Toast, Tooltip) belong above all platforms, or does every platform fork them?
  • Authoring discipline — build shape-specific first and generalize on second use (lazy), or pre-spec Foundations (eager)?
  • Are L3 "screen recipes" first-class artifacts in the kit, or just illustrations in docs?

See 09-open-questions.md for full context.
