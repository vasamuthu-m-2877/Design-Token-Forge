Kit picker — platform × product-shape

Closes WT findings A2, A3 — v0.1 picker was a desktop-shape grid

What changed: v0.1's picker showed 6 cards (Dashboard / Marketing / Editorial / Social / Editor / Workflow) all implicitly desktop-shape. Mobile and marketing products had no path. v0.2 reframes as two stages: pick platform first, then pick product shape within. Each picked combination loads a specific Foundation + shape-kit pair.

Annotations

  • Stage 1 + Stage 2 split makes shape = platform × product-shape explicit.
  • Removes desktop bias of v0.1. Mobile is no longer second-class.
  • "Ready" pill on Writing + Commerce reflects Phase 2.5b/2.5c deliverables. "Coming" shapes are honest about roadmap state.
  • Each card surfaces the build cost: "Mobile Foundation + N L2s" makes the Foundation tier visible to the designer at the picking moment.
  • Mobile platform card mentions iOS + Android together — platform-fork policy (NavigationBar vs AppBar) handled inside the Foundation, not at picker level.
